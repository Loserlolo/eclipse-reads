Chapter 13

The teasing started the next morning.

And it never stopped.

The moment Do-hyun walked into the café, Min-jun looked up from the register and grinned.

"Your boyfriend coming today?"

Do-hyun nearly dropped the box he was carrying.

"He is not my boyfriend."

"That's not a no."

"It literally was."

Min-jun laughed.

Unfortunately, several other coworkers heard the conversation.

Which made everything worse.

Much worse.

By noon, Do-hyun was ready to quit his job and move to another city.

Possibly another country.

Maybe another planet.

Anything to escape the constant teasing.

Then the bell above the café door chimed.

The room immediately became quieter.

Not because customers had stopped talking.

Because every employee suddenly looked toward the entrance.

Seo-yul walked inside.

Smiling as usual.

Do-hyun wanted to disappear.

Immediately.

Min-jun looked between them.

Then grinned even wider.

"Wow."

"Don't."

"I didn't say anything."

"You were about to."

"I definitely was."

Seo-yul approached the counter.

"What did I miss?"

“Nothing.”

"Something."

Seo-yul looked amused.

Do-hyun looked exhausted.

Min-jun looked delighted.

It was a terrible combination.

The rest of the afternoon passed surprisingly quickly.

Mostly because Seo-yul stayed.

Again.

At this point nobody believed he was visiting for the coffee anymore.

Not even Do-hyun.

When the shift finally ended, Seo-yul was waiting outside.

As usual.

The evening sky glowed orange and purple above the city.

The streets felt peaceful.

Comfortable.

The two of them walked side by side.

For a while neither spoke.

Then Seo-yul glanced over.

"Your coworkers hate me."

Do-hyun laughed.

The sound escaped before he could stop it.

"No."

"Then why were they all staring at me?"

Do-hyun immediately looked away.

"Don't worry about it."

"Oh no."

Seo-yul smiled.

"Now I'm definitely worried about it."

Do-hyun groaned.

The smile on Seo-yul's face only grew.

And somehow, despite all the teasing, despite all the embarrassment, despite everything...

Do-hyun found himself smiling too.

Without even realizing it.

Seo-yul noticed immediately.

Of course he did.

"There it is."

"There what is?"

"That smile."

Do-hyun rolled his eyes.

But this time he couldn't stop smiling.

And for once...

He didn't really want to.
