Chapter 1

Do-hyun hated slow nights.

Busy nights gave him something to focus on. Orders piled up, customers complained, and there was never enough time to think.

Slow nights were different. Slow nights left him alone with his thoughts.

And lately those thoughts always seemed to circle back to the same person.

Seo-yul.

The café was quiet enough that he could hear the clock ticking above the register.

He hated that.

Because silence always made him think.

And thinking always led back to Seo-yul.
